— Opening

Good Morning,
Everyone.

My name is Ishaan Singh and I'm standing for Principal's Representative as my first choice, and Radio Recess Coordinator as my second.

For those who don't know me, I wake up at 5am everyday to work on my non-profit startup, volunteer at an animal shelter, and have researched at highly praised companies.

And for those who do know me... please remain quiet.

On a more serious note, over the past few years I’ve taken part in many activities in and outside school. Last year, I was a project manager working with a company to develop an original solution, where we placed 4th. I’ve also worked behind the scenes in events like Theatrical Journey, where we came 1st. These experiences have shown me that small, consistent efforts create real impact.

— On Leadership

What Leadership
Means to Me

To me, a good leader doesn’t just give instructions- they listen, understand, and represent others fairly. Leadership is about responsibility, communication, and staying calm under pressure, and I believe I bring these qualities.

— To The Teachers and Staff

Dear
Teachers,

I want to whole heartedly thank you for all the work that you do behind the scenes.

As Principal's Representative, I'll always come to you with issues framed constructively- not as complaints, but as problems I want to solve collaboratively. I'll be respectful of the challenges you face too, not just the ones students bring to me.

As Radio Recess Coordinator, I'll ensure everything I run is appropriate, inclusive, and adds to the school's culture rather than disrupting it. I want the broadcast to be something you look forward to too.

🤝

Collaborative, Not Confrontational

Every issue I bring forward will come with a proposed solution, not just a grievance. I respect your time.

👋

Consistent & Reliable

You won't have to chase me. I'll show up to every commitment, every forum, every broadcast on time.

📢

Appropriate Content Always

Radio Recess will be student-energising but never inappropriate. I'll always run themes past the relevant staff first.

📋

Transparent Reporting

I'll keep relevant staff updated on what I'm hearing from students- so there are no surprises from either side.

ISHAAN SINGH.

Principal's Rep • Radio Recess Coordinator- Student Council 2026
